## How Ketho resources are produced

- Dumped via **KethoDoc** from a running WoW client.
- **GlobalStrings** and **AtlasInfo** may be sourced from [wago.tools](https://wago.tools/).
- Templates/mixins parsed from FrameXML (see Ketho WowDoc tooling).
